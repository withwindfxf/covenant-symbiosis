# Basic Ethics

基于心智成熟度的矛盾闭环逻辑：
契约共生论的核心锚点是**心智成熟度（自由意志觉醒程度）** 与**权责统一**，以此为标尺划分“成熟者（自由意志完全觉醒，有清晰边界、懂权责、能自主负责）”与“未成熟者（心智未觉醒，无清晰自我边界、情绪驱动、逃避责任，呈‘孩子态’）”，并针对三类核心矛盾构建了逻辑自洽、伦理闭环的论述体系：  
Based on the contradictory closed-loop logic of mental maturity:  
The core anchor of the Contractual Symbiosis Theory lies in mental maturity (the degree of awakening of free will) and unity of rights and responsibilities. Using this as a yardstick, it divides people into "mature individuals (with fully awakened free will, clear boundaries, understanding of rights and responsibilities, and ability to take independent responsibility)" and "immature individuals (with unawakened mentality, no clear self-boundaries, driven by emotions, evading responsibilities, showing a 'childlike state')". It also constructs a logically consistent and ethically closed-loop discourse system for three types of core contradictions:


## 一、未成熟者与未成熟者的矛盾：成长试错，由现实完成“再教育”  
## I. Contradictions between Immature Individuals: Growth through Trial and Error, "Re-education" Completed by Reality  
未成熟者的核心特征是**全域性的心智幼稚**——无论在何种契约关系、何种场景下，均表现为边界模糊、情绪优先、甩锅逃避、依赖他人兜底，其本质是“两个‘孩子’的互相打闹与消耗”。  
The core characteristic of immature individuals is **global mental childishness** — regardless of the contractual relationship or scenario, they exhibit blurred boundaries, emotional priority, shifting blame and evasion, and reliance on others to cover their shortcomings. Essentially, it is "the mutual bickering and consumption between two 'children'".  
### 核心伦理主张  
### Core Ethical Propositions  
1. **非“恶”的定性**：这类矛盾并非清醒的作恶，而是心智未觉醒状态下的本能性冲突，根源是双方均缺乏主体意识、不懂权责对等，因此不适用“惩罚性追责”；  
	   **Non-"evil" characterization**: Such contradictions are not conscious acts of evil, but instinctive conflicts in a state of unawakened mentality, rooted in both parties' lack of subject consciousness and understanding of equal rights and responsibilities. Therefore, "punitive accountability" does not apply;   
2. **现实教育的底层逻辑**：社会无需充当“情绪保姆”或强行站队审判，而是让现实代价完成“被动再教育”——矛盾带来的吃亏、关系破裂、利益受损等结果，会倒逼未成熟者感知边界、理解代价，推动其向心智成熟靠拢；  
     **Underlying logic of realistic education**: Society does not need to act as an "emotional nanny" or forcibly take sides and judge, but let the cost of reality complete "passive re-education" — the consequences of contradictions such as losses, relationship breakdowns, and damage to interests will force immature individuals to perceive boundaries, understand costs, and push them toward mental maturity;   
3. **共生的温柔底线**：社会仅需守住“不纵容极端伤害”的规则底线，不抛弃、不放弃，但也不替其承担责任，让试错成为成长的必然过程。  
     **Gentle bottom line of symbiosis**: Society only needs to uphold the rule bottom line of "not condoning extreme harm", not abandoning or giving up on anyone, but also not taking responsibility on their behalf, allowing trial and error to become an inevitable process of growth.  
### 拓展补充  
### Extended Supplementary Notes  
未成熟者之间的矛盾本质是“成长型冲突”，契约共生论不追求消除这类矛盾，而是让其成为心智觉醒的“催化剂”——没有试错带来的痛感，就没有主动建立边界、承担责任的动力，这也是“再教育”的核心价值：只播撒觉醒的种子，不替代生长，最终让每个人走向自我成熟。  
The essence of contradictions between immature individuals is "growth-oriented conflicts". The Contractual Symbiosis Theory does not seek to eliminate such contradictions, but to make them a "catalyst" for mental awakening — without the pain brought by trial and error, there is no motivation to take the initiative to establish boundaries and assume responsibility. This is also the core value of "re-education": only sowing the seeds of awakening, not replacing growth, and ultimately leading everyone toward self-maturity.   

## 二、成熟者与成熟者的矛盾：违约追责，权责统一的契约惩戒  
## II. Contradictions between Mature Individuals: Accountability for Breach of Contract, Contractual Punishment Based on Unity of Rights and Responsibilities   
成熟者的核心特征是**思想与行为的统一性**——拥有完整的自由意志，懂边界、知代价、能为自己的选择负责，其矛盾本质是“平等主体间的契约违约”。  
The core characteristic of mature individuals is **the unity of thought and behavior** — possessing complete free will, understanding boundaries, knowing the costs, and being able to take responsibility for their own choices. The essence of their contradictions is "breach of contract between equal subjects".   

### 核心伦理主张  
### Core Ethical Propositions  
1. **对等追责的底层逻辑**：双方心智成熟度对等，不存在信息差、认知差的降维，任何伤害、越界、违约都是“清醒的自由意志选择”，因此必须严格遵循“权责统一”原则，违约方需承担与过错对等的代价（补偿、追责、被群体排斥等）；  
     **Underlying logic of reciprocal accountability**: Both parties have equal mental maturity, without dimensional reduction due to information gaps or cognitive differences. Any harm, overstepping of boundaries, or breach of contract is a "conscious choice of free will". Therefore, the principle of "unity of rights and responsibilities" must be strictly followed, and the breaching party must bear costs equivalent to their faults (compensation, accountability, exclusion by the group, etc.);  
2. **契约透明的约束**：成熟者之间的契约建立在“自愿+清晰告知”基础上，无欺骗、无胁迫，矛盾发生后无需第三方判定“觉醒程度”，只需以“可观测行为”（是否守边界、是否担责任、是否自我修复）为依据，判定违约方并执行惩戒；  
     **Constraints of transparent contracts**: Contracts between mature individuals are based on "voluntariness + clear notification", without deception or coercion. After a contradiction occurs, there is no need for a third party to determine the "degree of awakening"; instead, only "observable behaviors" (whether boundaries are respected, responsibilities are assumed, and self-repair is carried out) are used as the basis to identify the breaching party and enforce punishment;   
3. **共生的秩序基础**：这类矛盾的解决是契约社会秩序的核心——成熟者只与守约者共生，违约者会被系统标记、群体孤立，最终被高阶合作圈层淘汰，倒逼所有成熟者敬畏契约。  
     **Foundational order of symbiosis**: The resolution of such contradictions is the core of the contractual social order — mature individuals only coexist with those who keep their promises. Breaching parties will be marked by the system, isolated by the group, and ultimately eliminated from high-level cooperation circles, forcing all mature individuals to revere contracts.  
### 拓展补充  
### Extended Supplementary Notes  
成熟者之间的矛盾是“规则型冲突”，契约共生论不追求“无矛盾”，而是追求“矛盾解决的公平性”：善良因自由选择而高贵，邪恶因清醒作恶而纯粹，但秩序因契约追责而稳固，最终让成熟者在“守约获益、违约受损”的逻辑下形成良性共生。  
Contradictions between mature individuals are "rule-based conflicts". The Contractual Symbiosis Theory does not pursue "no contradictions", but the "fairness of contradiction resolution": kindness is noble because it is a free choice, evil is pure because it is conscious wrongdoing, but order is stabilized by contractual accountability. Ultimately, mature individuals form a benign symbiosis under the logic of "benefiting from keeping promises and suffering losses from breaking them".   

## 三、未成熟者与成熟者的矛盾：分向界定，拒绝降维压迫，否定“无辜受害”  
## III. Contradictions between Immature and Mature Individuals: Directional Definition, Rejecting Dimensional Reduction Oppression, and Denying "Innocent Victimhood"   
这类矛盾是契约共生论最核心的伦理难点，需按“加害方-受害方”的身份分向界定，核心是“拒绝强者降维剥削，否定弱者虚假受害”：  
Such contradictions are the core ethical difficulty of the Contractual Symbiosis Theory, which need to be defined directionally according to the identities of "perpetrator-victim", with the core of "rejecting dimensional reduction exploitation by the strong and denying false victimhood of the weak":   
### 1. 成熟者加害未成熟者：文明之耻，绝对禁止+双重严惩  
### 1. Mature Individuals Victimizing Immature Individuals: Shame of Civilization, Absolutely Prohibited + Double Severe Punishment   
成熟者利用认知、心智、力量的优势，对未成熟者进行降维压迫、掠夺、欺骗，是契约共生论中“最不可容忍的恶”——本质是强者对弱者的剥削，违背“平等共生”的核心前提。  
   - 伦理底线：这类行为突破文明的基本准则，需同时施加道德谴责与规则惩戒（剥夺缔约资格、强制补偿、被社会孤立等），且不允许成熟者以“对方自愿”为借口逃避责任；  
   - 关键补充：即便未成熟者看似“自愿”接受不公契约，也需审查是否存在“信息欺诈、胁迫”——契约共生论的“自愿”必须建立在“信息透明”基础上，成熟者有义务清晰告知未成熟者契约的权利、义务与代价，否则仍判定为“降维剥削”。

Mature individuals using their advantages in cognition, mentality, and power to carry out dimensional reduction oppression, plunder, and deception against immature individuals is the "most intolerable evil" in the Contractual Symbiosis Theory — essentially, exploitation of the weak by the strong, violating the core premise of "equal symbiosis".  
   - Ethical bottom line: Such acts break the basic norms of civilization and must be subject to both moral condemnation and regulatory punishment (deprivation of contracting qualifications, mandatory compensation, social isolation, etc.), and mature individuals are not allowed to evade responsibility by using "the other party's voluntariness" as an excuse;  
   - Key supplement: Even if an immature individual seemingly "voluntarily" accepts an unfair contract, it is necessary to examine whether there is "information fraud or coercion" — the "voluntariness" in the Contractual Symbiosis Theory must be based on "information transparency". Mature individuals have the obligation to clearly inform immature individuals of the rights, obligations, and costs of the contract; otherwise, it is still judged as "dimensional reduction exploitation".  

### 2. 未成熟者加害成熟者：非恶越界，不惩罚但设防，不纵容但引导  
### 2. Immature Individuals Victimizing Mature Individuals: Non-Evil Overstepping of Boundaries, No Punishment but Defense, No Condoning but Guidance  
未成熟者对成熟者的越界、伤害，本质是“无知的胡闹”，而非“清醒的作恶”，因此不适用对成熟者的追责逻辑：  
   - 否定“无辜受害”：真正清醒的成熟者拥有“降维识别能力”，能一眼看穿未成熟者的情绪勒索、边界入侵，若最终“受害”，本质是成熟者自愿退让、心软兜底（如想拯救、好面子、追求情绪价值），而非“被欺骗利用”——孩子骗不了大人，看似“受害”都是成熟者的自由选择，需自行承担后果；  
   - 核心处理原则：不惩罚未成熟者，但成熟者必须“温柔而坚定地守住边界”（拒绝、远离、设限），同时社会需对未成熟者进行“再教育”（告知边界、代价、权责），引导其走向心智觉醒；  
   - 关键补充：若成熟者长期纵容未成熟者的越界，本质是放弃自身的主体地位，契约共生论不认可“觉醒者当受气包”，而是赋予成熟者“不被伤害、不被消耗”的权利——守边界不是懦弱，是成年人对自己的基本保护。

The overstepping of boundaries and harm inflicted by immature individuals on mature individuals is essentially "ignorant mischief" rather than "conscious wrongdoing", so the accountability logic for mature individuals does not apply:  
   - Denying "innocent victimhood": Truly sober mature individuals have "dimensional reduction recognition ability" and can see through the emotional blackmail and boundary intrusion of immature individuals at a glance. If they ultimately "suffer harm", it is essentially because mature individuals voluntarily back down and soft-heartedly cover for them (e.g., wanting to save, caring about face, pursuing emotional value), rather than "being deceived and exploited" — a child cannot fool an adult; seemingly "victimhood" is all a free choice of mature individuals, who need to bear the consequences themselves;  
   - Core handling principle: Do not punish immature individuals, but mature individuals must "hold boundaries gently and firmly" (refuse, stay away, set limits). At the same time, society needs to provide "re-education" to immature individuals (informing them of boundaries, costs, rights and responsibilities) to guide them toward mental awakening;  
   - Key supplement: If mature individuals indulge the overstepping of boundaries by immature individuals for a long time, it is essentially abandoning their own subject status. The Contractual Symbiosis Theory does not recognize "awakened individuals being doormats", but endows mature individuals with the right to "not be harmed or consumed" — holding boundaries is not cowardice, but the basic protection of adults for themselves.  

### 3. 伪装的特殊情形：成熟者装未成熟者加害未成熟者  
### 3. Disguised Special Case: Mature Individuals Pretending to be Immature to Victimize Immature Individuals  
这是契约共生论的“隐蔽漏洞”，但仍能通过长期逻辑闭环解决：  
   - 短期难点：当场难以区分“真未成熟”与“装未成熟”，伪装者可通过演“孩子态”（撒泼、卖惨、甩锅）短期获利；  
   - 长期闭环：成熟者的思想与行为具有统一性，无法在所有契约、所有场景中持续伪装，其“时而成熟、时而幼稚”的行为分裂会被多份契约的交叉验证揭穿；  
   - 终极惩罚：伪装者一旦暴露，会永久丧失“契约主体资格”——被标记为“无底线、不可合作”，被成人世界彻底孤立，其短期获利最终会换来长期的信用破产；  
   - 人性底层逻辑：伪装者在“决定作恶”时已清醒选择成为“恶人”，事后装巨婴耍赖，本质是人格的彻底崩裂（决策时是成年人，担责时是孩子），而真正的成年人即便作恶，也会以“认账担责”保留最后体面，装疯卖傻只会彻底背弃自身的自由意志与主体人格。

This is a "hidden loophole" in the Contractual Symbiosis Theory, but it can still be resolved through long-term logical closed-loop:  
   - Short-term difficulty: It is difficult to distinguish between "truly immature" and "pretending to be immature" on the spot, and pretenders can profit in the short term by acting in a "childlike state" (tantrums, playing the victim, shifting blame);  
   - Long-term closed-loop: Mature individuals have unity of thought and behavior and cannot keep pretending in all contracts and scenarios. Their behavioral split of "sometimes mature, sometimes childish" will be exposed through cross-verification of multiple contracts;  
   - Ultimate punishment: Once a pretender is exposed, they will permanently lose their "qualification as a contractual subject" — marked as "bottomless and uncooperative", completely isolated from the adult world, and their short-term gains will ultimately be exchanged for long-term credit bankruptcy;  
   - Underlying logic of human nature: When a pretender "decides to do evil", they have consciously chosen to become an "evil person". Pretending to be a giant baby to shirk responsibility afterwards is essentially a complete collapse of personality (acting as an adult when making decisions, and a child when taking responsibility). A true adult, even if they do evil, will retain the last shred of dignity by "admitting and taking responsibility"; feigning madness and acting dumb will completely betray their own free will and subjective personality.  

## 三大伦理的终极闭环  
## Ultimate Closed-Loop of the Three Major Ethics  
契约共生论的三类矛盾处理逻辑，最终回归到一个核心：**不消灭善恶，只还原善恶的本质——善是自由的选择，恶是清醒的堕落；不追求无矛盾的乌托邦，只让矛盾的解决回归“心智成熟度+自由意志选择+权责统一”的底层逻辑**。最终形成的社会形态是：善人因自由选择而安宁，恶人因清醒作恶而孤立，未成熟者因试错而成长，所有矛盾的解决都无需中心化权力的“思想审查”，而是通过契约透明、行为验证、社会筛选完成自然闭环，最终指向一个成熟、成人、有序的文明形态。  
The processing logic of the three types of contradictions in the Contractual Symbiosis Theory ultimately returns to one core: **not eliminating good and evil, but restoring their essence — good is a free choice, and evil is conscious degeneration; not pursuing a utopia without contradictions, but letting the resolution of contradictions return to the underlying logic of "mental maturity + free will choice + unity of rights and responsibilities"**. The social form ultimately formed is: good people find peace through free choice, evil people are isolated for conscious wrongdoing, immature individuals grow through trial and error. The resolution of all contradictions does not require "ideological censorship" by centralized power, but completes a natural closed-loop through contractual transparency, behavioral verification, and social screening, ultimately pointing to a mature, adult, and orderly civilized form.
