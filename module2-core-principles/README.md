
## 核心定位 | Core Positioning

契约共生框架的底层逻辑基石，是解析我们现有问题与挑战、定义我们未来的目标与愿景的核心标尺。

The underlying logical cornerstone of the Contract Symbiosis Framework is the core benchmark for analyzing our existing problems and challenges, and defining our future goals and visions.

---

## 1. 自由意志主体 | Free Will Subject

### 定义 | Definition

人是拥有独立、清醒、不可剥夺的自由意志的核心主体，所有社会规则的设计均以尊重并激活个体自由意志为前提。

Humans are core subjects with independent, conscious, and inalienable free will, and the design of all social rules is premised on respecting and activating individual free will.

### 我们现有问题与挑战 | Our Existing Problems and Challenges

- 自由意志被压抑：外部力量以集体、道德、规训为名，替代或绑架个体自主决策；

- Free will is suppressed: external forces replace or kidnap individual autonomous decision-making in the name of collectivism, morality, or discipline;

- 主体意识缺失：个体依附于外部规则或他人意志，陷入「被动生存」状态，逃避自我责任；

- Lack of subjective awareness: individuals rely on external rules or others' will, fall into a state of "passive existence", and evade self-responsibility;

- 选择非自主：决策基于盲从、情绪或利益裹挟，而非理性与本心。

- Choices are not autonomous: decisions are based on blind obedience, emotions, or interest coercion, rather than reason and inner conviction.

### 我们未来的目标与愿景 | Our Future Goals and Visions

- 教育核心转向「主体觉醒」：培养个体「清醒认知、自主决策、自我负责」的核心能力；

- The core of education shifts to "subject awakening": cultivating individuals' core abilities of "clear cognition, independent decision-making, and self-responsibility";

- 规则设计保障「选择自由」：不预设统一标准答案，只保障个体决策的空间与权利；

- Rule design guarantees "freedom of choice": no uniform standard answers are preset, only the space and rights for individual decision-making are guaranteed;

- 社会氛围尊重「多元意志」：摒弃价值审判，认可不同自主选择的合理性。

- Social atmosphere respects "diverse wills": abandoning value judgment and recognizing the rationality of different autonomous choices.

## 2. 权责统一 | Unity of Rights and Responsibilities

### 定义 | Definition

权利与责任高度对等，享有何种权利必须匹配相应责任，无「只享权利不担责任」的特权，也无「只担责任无权利」的压迫。这一切的前提，是个体以清醒的自由意志自愿进入，主动承接相应的权利与责任，而非被动裹挟、被迫承担。

Rights and responsibilities are highly commensurate; the enjoyment of any right must be matched with corresponding responsibilities, with no privilege of "enjoying rights without assuming responsibilities" and no oppression of "assuming responsibilities without rights". All of this is premised on individuals voluntarily entering with a clear free will, taking the initiative to undertake the corresponding rights and responsibilities, rather than being coerced or forced to bear them.

### 我们现有问题与挑战 | Our Existing Problems and Challenges

- 权责撕裂：少数群体占据核心权利，却将责任与代价转移给多数人；

- Tearing of rights and responsibilities: a minority group occupies core rights but shifts responsibilities and costs to the majority;

- 责任甩锅：以道德、情感、集体为名，将个体选择的后果推诿给他人或集体；

- Shirking responsibilities: shifting the consequences of individual choices to others or the collective in the name of morality, emotion, or collectivism;

- 特权固化：身份、阶层、资源差异导致权责规则双标，公平性缺失。

- Privilege solidification: differences in identity, class, and resources lead to double standards in rights and responsibility rules, resulting in lack of fairness.

### 我们未来的目标与愿景 | Our Future Goals and Visions

- 规则刚性化：以契约替代道德自觉，明确权责边界，违约必有对等代价；

- Rigid rules: replacing moral consciousness with contracts, clarifying the boundaries of rights and responsibilities, and ensuring that breaches of contract carry commensurate costs;

- 公平普适化：消除身份特权，权责匹配规则统一适用于所有个体与群体；

- Fair and universal: eliminating identity privileges, and the rules of rights-responsibility matching apply uniformly to all individuals and groups;

- 责任可追溯：决策与选择的责任归属清晰，避免「集体负责=无人负责」。

- Traceable responsibilities: clear attribution of responsibilities for decisions and choices, avoiding "collective responsibility = no one is responsible".

## 3. 契约边界 | Contract Boundary

### 定义 | Definition

个体、群体、组织之间存在清晰、可执行、可验证的行为边界，「我的」与「你的」界限明确，不侵犯他人边界，也不容他人侵犯自身边界。这种边界的建立，以个体自由意志自愿进入为前提，边界的核心意义是约束——约束所有自愿进入者的行为，杜绝越界、掠夺与内耗，让每一份相处都有底线、有分寸。

There are clear, enforceable, and verifiable behavioral boundaries between individuals, groups, and organizations. The line between "mine" and "yours" is clear: do not infringe on others' boundaries, and do not tolerate others infringing on one's own boundaries. The establishment of such boundaries is premised on individuals voluntarily entering with their free will, and the core significance of boundaries is restraint — restraining the behavior of all voluntary entrants, eliminating crossing boundaries, plunder and internal friction, so that every interaction has a bottom line and a sense of proportion.

### 我们现有问题与挑战 | Our Existing Problems and Challenges

- 边界模糊化：以人情、关系、道德替代规则，导致行为边界无明确标准；

- Blurred boundaries: replacing rules with favoritism, relationships, and morality, resulting in no clear standards for behavioral boundaries;

- 越界无代价：强者可随意侵犯弱者边界，越界行为缺乏纠错与惩罚机制；

- No cost for crossing boundaries: the strong can arbitrarily infringe on the boundaries of the weak, and there is no correction or punishment mechanism for boundary-crossing behavior;

- 边界双标化：对自身宽松、对他人严苛，双重标准加剧冲突与内耗。

- Double standards for boundaries: lenient to oneself and harsh to others, double standards exacerbate conflicts and internal friction.

### 我们未来的目标与愿景 | Our Future Goals and Visions

- 边界显性化：将隐性的人际、利益边界转化为显性契约，减少模糊地带；

- Explicit boundaries: converting implicit interpersonal and interest boundaries into explicit contracts to reduce ambiguous areas;

- 越界有惩戒：建立可执行的纠错与惩罚机制，保障边界不可侵犯；

- Punishment for crossing boundaries: establishing an enforceable correction and punishment mechanism to ensure that boundaries are inviolable;

- 边界协商化：边界可通过双方自愿协商调整，而非单方面强制修改。

- Negotiable boundaries: boundaries can be adjusted through voluntary negotiation between parties, rather than unilaterally forced modification.

## 4. 平等共生 | Equal Symbiosis

### 定义 | Definition

摒弃掠夺、内卷、零和博弈的生存模式，以平等、合作、共赢为核心，构建「人人如龙」的舒展型社会，核心是「不损害他人，也不被他人损害」。

Abandon the survival mode of plunder, involution, and zero-sum games, and build a stretched society where all people can rise to their full potential with equality, cooperation, and win-win as the core, with the essence of "not harming others, nor being harmed by others."

### 我们现有问题与挑战 | Our Existing Problems and Challenges

- 掠夺式生存：少数人通过剥夺他人利益实现自身收益，加剧资源分配失衡；

- Predatory survival: a few people realize their own gains by depriving others of interests, exacerbating the imbalance in resource distribution;

- 内卷化竞争：所有人陷入无增量的内耗式竞争，消耗个体创造力与幸福感；

- Involutionary competition: everyone falls into incremental-free internal friction competition, consuming individual creativity and happiness;

- 价值单一化：以权力、财富为唯一价值标尺，压抑多元生存与创造方式。

- Monolithic values: taking power and wealth as the sole value criterion, suppressing diverse ways of survival and creation.

### 我们未来的目标与愿景 | Our Future Goals and Visions

- 价值多元化：尊重创造、服务、思考等不同生存方式的平等价值；

- Diversified values: respecting the equal value of different survival methods such as creation, service, and thinking;

- 反掠夺机制化：以规则阻断「不劳而获、掠夺他人」的路径，保障收益与贡献匹配；

- Institutionalized anti-plunder: using rules to block the path of "reaping without sowing and plundering others", ensuring that gains match contributions;

- 低内耗社会：消除无意义的竞争焦虑，让个体专注自我成长与价值创造。

- Low internal friction society: eliminating meaningless competitive anxiety and allowing individuals to focus on self-growth and value creation.

## 总结 | Summary

契约共生的成长之路，始于向内的清醒审视——而自由意志的第一次真正行使，绝非天然产生，更离不开以“尊重并激活个体自由意志”为核心的引导与滋养。这种引导并非强硬的灌输，而是以尊重个体为前提，通过深刻的自我审视，帮助我们建立起清晰的权责认知、明确的边界认知、正向的共生认知，三者兼具、互为支撑，真正的自由意志才能得以孕育、生根。

The growth path of contract symbiosis starts with an inward and clear self-examination — and the first true exercise of free will is by no means naturally occurring, but rather relies on guidance and nourishment centered on "respecting and activating individual free will". This guidance is not a rigid indoctrination, but takes respecting the individual as the premise, helping us establish clear cognition of rights and responsibilities, clear cognition of boundaries, and positive cognition of symbiosis through in-depth self-examination, so that true free will can take root and grow.

尤为重要的是，未经过这种引导激活的自由意志，不能算是合法的自由意志——它往往是盲目的、无边界的，既无法对自身行为负责，也会忽视他人的边界与权益。没有这种以激活为核心的引导，我们无法自然而然地形成成熟的认知，更无法拥有清醒、自主、合法的自由意志。

It is particularly important that free will that has not been activated through such guidance cannot be regarded as legitimate free will — it is often blind and boundless, unable to take responsibility for one's own actions, and also ignores the boundaries and rights of others. Without such guidance centered on activation, we cannot naturally form mature cognition, let alone possess clear, autonomous and legitimate free will.

当我们在这种引导下完成自我审视，明晰自身权责、守住行为边界、认同共生理念，才能真正锚定自由意志的核心，主动扛起自身责任，践行权责统一的底色，进而学会尊重他人边界，让平等共生的理念从自我成长延伸至与人相处、与群体共生。

When we complete self-examination under such guidance, clarify our own rights and responsibilities, hold our behavioral boundaries, and recognize the concept of symbiosis, we can truly anchor the core of free will, take the initiative to take our own responsibilities, practice the essence of the unity of rights and responsibilities, and then learn to respect the boundaries of others, extending the concept of symbiosis from self-growth to getting along with others and coexisting with groups.

这不是终点，而是更广阔成长的正向循环的起点：当自由意志被完全激活，便无需再进行重复引导，我们将以更通透的自我审视，坚守权责担当，维护多元边界，推动平等共生的理念从个体延伸至更广阔的范围，形成“引导激活→自我审视→认知提升→自由意志成熟→正向成长”的良性循环。

This is not the end, but the starting point for a positive cycle of broader growth: when free will is fully activated, there is no need for repeated guidance. We will, with a more thorough self-examination, uphold the responsibility of rights and obligations, safeguard diverse boundaries, and promote the concept of equal symbiosis from individuals to a wider scope, forming a virtuous cycle of "guidance and activation → self-examination → cognitive improvement → maturity of free will → positive growth".

我们终将一步步奔赴预想的理想图景：一个人人拥有合法自由意志、权责清晰、边界分明、平等共生的舒展社会，每一个人都能在其中坚守自我、实现价值，彼此尊重、彼此成就，告别内耗与掠夺，共赴更具温度与希望的未来。

We will eventually move step by step towards the ideal picture we envision: a relaxed society where everyone has legitimate free will, clear rights and responsibilities, distinct boundaries, and equal symbiosis. Everyone can uphold themselves, realize their value, respect each other, achieve each other, bid farewell to internal friction and plunder, and move towards a warmer and more hopeful future together.
