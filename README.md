### 项目迭代说明 / Project Iteration Note
本项目处于持续的思想迭代中，代码/设计会随认知更新调整，核心是探索**基于共生契约论四大核心支柱（自由意志、权责统一、契约边界、平等共生）的「自愿为起点、契约明权责、共生为归宿」的协作与相处模式** 方向的可能性——我们不追求一蹴而就的完美框架，而是以开放、迭代的姿态，在实践中打磨适配不同场景的共生契约解决方案，让“减少内耗、共生共赢”的核心思想真正落地。

This project is in a continuous process of ideological iteration, and the code/design will be adjusted with the update of cognition. Its core is to explore the possibilities of **collaboration and interaction models based on the four core pillars of Symbiotic Contract Theory (Free Will, Unity of Rights and Responsibilities, Contract Boundaries, Equal Symbiosis), which take "voluntariness as the starting point, contracts to clarify rights and responsibilities, and symbiosis as the destination"** — we do not pursue a perfect framework achieved overnight, but adopt an open and iterative attitude to polish symbiotic contract solutions adapted to different scenarios in practice, so that the core idea of "reducing internal friction and achieving symbiosis and win-win results" can be truly implemented.

### 中文版

### 一、从每个人的切身痛感说起
我们这一代人，在生活里普遍被同一种不安笼罩：

在一段关系里，
**我不知道自己要付出什么，也不知道能换来什么。**

在家庭与婚姻中，
**我不知道责任该怎么划分，也不知道权益该如何保障。**

在生育与养育面前，
**我不知道自己要承担多少，也不知道能获得什么支撑。**

在社会里努力生活，
**我不知道付出会换来什么结果，也不知道底线在哪里。**

一切都模糊、浮动、不可预期。
因为**权责不明晰**，所以人心不安；
因为不安，所以互相防备、互相消耗、不敢投入、不敢信任。

这不是某个人的问题，
而是所有人共同的生存困境。

### 二、核心问题：一切痛苦，源于权责不明
我们真正要解决的，是现代社会三个最底层、最贴身的死结：

1. **关系权责不明 → 婚恋互害、信任崩塌**
   双方都不清楚彼此的义务与边界，付出不对等，期待不清晰，最终互相伤害、恐惧亲密、选择退缩。

2. **家庭权责不明 → 生育恐惧、代际紧绷**
   生育、养育、教育、养老的成本与责任高度模糊，过度集中在个体与小家庭，让人不敢生、不愿生、生了也焦虑。

3. **社会权责不明 → 个体疲惫、预期不稳**
   上下权责不清，规则弹性过大，个体看不清长期保障，于是不敢付出、不敢相信、不愿参与。

所有困境，归根到底一句话：
**我不清楚自己要付出什么，也不确定自己能得到什么。**

### 三、思想渊源
我们以历史为镜，取其精神，去其形式：
- 向往周代秩序的柔和：**内安百姓，各安其位**
- 警戒秦代过度管控：**管得越多，人心越乱**
- 遵循刘邦约法三章的极简：**规则少、边界清、不折腾、公信立**

由此，推导出一套现代、温和、可落地的秩序方案。

### 四、核心理念
1. 一切正当关系，始于**自愿**。
2. 一切稳定秩序，立于**契约**。
3. 一切主动付出，基于**保障**。
4. 一切长久共生，成于**守约**。
5. 一切制度设计，归于**人本**。

### 五、核心解决方案
**唯一的解药，是把权责重新说清楚：契约。**

契约的本质，就是一句话：
**你明确付出什么，就确定得到什么。**

我们以此构建一套全新的关系框架：
- 以**自愿**为起点
- 以**契约**明权责
- 以**保障**稳预期
- 以**守约**促合作
- 以**共生**为归宿

完整闭环：
**自愿 → 契约 → 权责清晰 → 安全感 → 自愿付出 → 彼此成就**

### 六、核心原则（不可动摇）
1. **自愿优先**：不强迫、不绑架、不胁迫。
2. **契约至上**：用清晰约定代替模糊人情。
3. **权责对等**：付出与获得必须可预期、相对称。
4. **保障前置**：先让人安心，人才愿意尽责。
5. **人本为终**：制度服务于人，而非人服从制度。

### 七、最终目标
我们希望建立一个：
- 每个人都清楚**自己该付出什么**
- 每个人都确定**自己能得到什么**
- 婚恋不再是赌局
- 生育不再是孤注一掷
- 付出不被消耗，善良不被辜负

的**契约共生社会**。

### 八、开源共建
本项目开放、开源、面向全球。
- 欢迎在核心逻辑内**细化、补充、落地、实践**
- 不可动摇底层共识：**自愿 · 契约 · 权责清晰 · 共生**
- 无领袖、无门户，只为构建一个更安稳、更温柔、更值得信任的人间秩序

## English Version

### 1. The Universal Human Anxiety
We live with a quiet, shared unease:

In relationships:
**I do not know what I must give, nor what I can safely expect to receive.**

In marriage and family:
**I do not know where my duties end, nor where my rights begin.**

Facing childbirth and raising children:
**I do not know how much I will sacrifice, nor what support I will have.**

In daily life and effort:
**I do not know if my work will be rewarded, nor where the bottom line lies.**

Everything is vague, floating, unpredictable.
**Unclear rights and duties breed insecurity.
Insecurity breeds defensiveness, exhaustion, and withdrawal.**

This is not personal failure.
It is a shared structural condition.

### 2. Core Problem
All suffering comes down to one thing:
**Unclear rights and responsibilities.**

We address three deeply personal, structural traps:

1. **Unclear duties in relationships**
   Without defined boundaries, partnership becomes exploitation, fear, and withdrawal.

2. **Unclear burdens in family & childbearing**
   Costs of life and aging fall unevenly on individuals, creating fear of commitment and parenthood.

3. **Unclear social accountability**
   Unstable rules and expectations leave people unwilling to contribute or trust.

All boil down to:
**I don’t know what I owe, and I don’t know what I’m owed.**

### 3. Intellectual Roots
We learn from history, not imitate it:
- The gentle order of early Zhou: **secure people, stable society**
- The warning of Qin overcontrol: **too much rule = too little trust**
- The simplicity of Liu Bang’s Three Laws: **clear rules, minimal interference, strong public trust**

From this, we build a modern, gentle, workable order.

### 4. Core Philosophy
1. Legitimate relationships begin with **voluntarism**.
2. Stable order rests on **covenant**.
3. Willing contribution comes from **security**.
4. Lasting coexistence relies on **promise-keeping**.
5. All systems exist for **humanity**.

### 5. Core Solution
**The only cure: clarity through covenant.**

Covenant means simply:
**You know what you give.
You know what you get.**

Our framework:
- **Voluntariness** as the starting point
- **Covenant** to define rights and duties
- **Security** to stabilize expectations
- **Promise-keeping** to enable cooperation
- **Symbiosis** as the goal

The closed loop:
**Voluntariness → Covenant → Clarity → Security → Contribution → Mutual flourishing**

### 6. Core Principles (Unalterable)
1. **Voluntarism First**
2. **Covenant as Foundation**
3. **Rights & Duties in Balance**
4. **Security Before Responsibility**
5. **Humanity as Purpose**

### 7. Ultimate Goal
A society where:
- Everyone understands **what they give**
- Everyone trusts **what they receive**
- Marriage is not a gamble
- Childbearing is not a lonely burden
- Effort is respected, kindness is protected

A **Covenant Symbiosis Society**.

### 8. Open Source Collaboration
Open to all, globally.
Refine, implement, discuss, and build.

- Core logic: **Voluntarism · Covenant · Clarity · Symbiosis**
- No leaders, no factions, no ownership
- For a more stable, gentle, and trustworthy world.
