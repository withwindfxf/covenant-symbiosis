### 模块1：核心定义 / Module 1: Core Definition
#### 1.1 核心定义【精简版】（适配框架快速查阅） / 1.1 Core Definition [Concise Version] (For quick reference in the framework)

共生契约论：以“共生共赢、减少内耗”为核心，以“彼此认可的底线共识”为支撑，是一套可落地、可复用、可扩展的解决方案式共识框架与指导工具——不搞学术玄学，不强制约束，适配个人、人际、组织等多场景，主打“普通人能用、能落地”，以开源模式迭代完善。

Symbiotic Contract Theory: With "symbiosis and win-win, reducing internal friction" as its core and "mutually recognized bottom-line consensus" as its support, it is a practical, reusable and extensible solution-oriented consensus framework and guidance tool — no academic metaphysics, no mandatory constraints, applicable to individuals, interpersonal relationships, organizations and other scenarios, focusing on "usability and implementability for ordinary people" and iteratively improved through an open-source model.

#### 1.2 核心定义【完整版】 / 1.2 Core Definition [Full Version]

核心定位：区别于纯学术理论，共生契约论是“解决实际相处/协作问题”的工具型共识框架，核心价值在于减少内耗、实现共生共赢，适配GitHub开源属性，欢迎所有人参与完善与实践。

Core Positioning: Different from pure academic theories, Symbiotic Contract Theory is a tool-based consensus framework for solving practical interpersonal and collaborative problems. Its core value lies in reducing internal friction and achieving symbiosis and win-win results. It fits GitHub's open-source attribute and welcomes everyone to participate in its improvement and practice.

##### （1）核心概念拆解（直白无冗余，避免歧义） / (1) Disassembly of Core Concepts (Straightforward and concise, avoiding ambiguity)

- 共生：核心是“理性善待、共存共赢”——不互相伤害、不单方面压榨，尊重彼此的生存与发展需求，不追求“无私奉献”，只追求“不内耗、共成长”，适配所有平等相处/协作场景。

- Symbiosis: Its core is "rational kindness and win-win coexistence" — no mutual harm, no one-sided exploitation, respect for each other's survival and development needs, no pursuit of "selfless dedication", but only "no internal friction and common growth", applicable to all equal interpersonal and collaborative scenarios.

- 契约：非法律式强制约定，而是“双方/多方自愿认可的底线共识”——明确自身底线、尊重他人底线，不越界、不推诿，简单可执行，可根据场景灵活调整细节。

- Contract: Not a legally mandatory agreement, but a "mutually recognized bottom-line consensus among two or more parties" — clarify your own bottom line, respect others' bottom lines, no overstepping or shirking, simple and implementable, with details flexibly adjusted according to scenarios.

- 共生契约论：以“共生”为核心导向，以“底线契约”为实施支撑，为个人、人际（家庭、朋友）、组织（职场、开源社群）提供可落地的相处/协作指导方法，是一套“温和但有力量”的工具型思想，而非强制约束。

- Symbiotic Contract Theory: Guided by "symbiosis" and supported by "bottom-line contracts", it provides practical interpersonal and collaborative guidance for individuals, interpersonal relationships (family, friends) and organizations (workplace, open-source communities). It is a "gentle but powerful" tool-based ideology, not a mandatory constraint.

① 不是学术理论：不堆砌专业术语，不追求理论完整性，所有定义均围绕“实用、可落地”展开，让普通人一看就懂、拿来就用；

① Not an academic theory: No redundant professional terms, no pursuit of theoretical completeness. All definitions focus on "practicality and implementability", making it easy for ordinary people to understand and use directly;

② 不是强制规则：不要求所有人完全遵循同一标准，核心是“共识”，底线之上可灵活适配不同场景、不同人群的需求；

② Not a mandatory rule: No requirement for everyone to follow the same standard; the core is "consensus", with flexible details adapted to different scenarios and groups above the bottom line;

③ 是开源工具框架：以GitHub开源思想为基础，鼓励使用者反馈问题、贡献案例，随实践不断迭代优化，让框架更贴合现实需求。

③ An open-source tool framework: Based on GitHub's open-source concept, it encourages users to feedback problems and contribute cases, and iteratively optimizes with practice to better meet real needs.
